Bridging Economies, Empowering Opportunities

Trade Co is a non-governmental, independent, and strategic facilitation platform that empowers economic, commercial, and policy-level engagement between India and the United States. With a focus on bilateral growth, we enable meaningful trade dialogues, international expansion, business matchmaking, and innovation partnerships.

Our mission is to create a trusted ecosystem that helps businesses, policymakers, and entrepreneurs thrive across borders—by breaking barriers, fostering trust, and promoting actionable collaboration.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution driving economic diplomacy, trade expansion, and innovation-led partnerships between India and the United States.

Our Mission

Facilitate Trade & Investment

Enable seamless trade and investment between Indian and American entities.

Strengthen Policy Frameworks

Drive bilateral cooperation through research-based advocacy and consultation.

Promote Innovation & Scaling

Foster entrepreneurship and innovation across borders for global competitiveness.

Support Cross-Industry Collaboration

Encourage synergy across sectors such as healthcare, tech, education, and energy.

Enable Business Engagement

Curate summits, delegations, and networking events for strategic partnerships.

What We Do

1. Trade Facilitation

Supporting Indian and U.S. businesses in navigating regulatory frameworks, market entry, export-import processes, and investment pathways.

2. Policy Engagement

Serving as a think tank and consultative body to drive regulatory harmonization, bilateral agreements, and economic diplomacy.

3. Business Development Support

Offering strategic advisory, partnership matchmaking, and expansion support for MSMEs, startups, and corporates in both countries.

4. Delegations & Events

Organizing official trade missions, roundtables, and flagship summits to foster direct engagement with stakeholders across both nations.

5. Research & Reports

Publishing industry reports, whitepapers, and policy notes on Indo-U.S. trade dynamics, opportunities, and global trends.
